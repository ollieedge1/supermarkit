Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a receipt?

Tap the scan button on the home screen, then point your camera at the receipt. Hold it steady and your monster will read the items automatically. You can edit anything after scanning.

Why did the scan miss some items?

Receipt scanning works best with clear, flat receipts in good lighting. If items are missing, you can add them manually using the edit feature. Crumpled or faded receipts may not scan as accurately.

How do I edit a scanned receipt?

Go to Spending → tap the receipt → tap the three-dot menu → Edit Receipt. You can change the store name, item names, categories, prices, and total.

How does household sharing work?

Create a household from Settings and share the 6-character invite code with your partner. Once they join, you'll both see each other's receipts, spending totals, and shopping lists in one place.

How does price tracking work?

When you scan the same item at the same store on different occasions, Supermarkit automatically detects if the price has changed and shows you the difference in the "What's Changed" section.

How do I set a budget?

On the home screen, tap "Set a budget" to create a weekly target. You'll see your progress throughout the week and get notified if you're approaching or exceeding your limit.

What are quests?

Quests are weekly challenges like "Beat last week's spend" or "Try a cheaper store." Complete them to earn XP and level up your monster. New quests are generated each time you scan.

How do shared shopping lists work?

Go to the Lists tab to create a shopping list. If you're in a household, lists are shared automatically — add an item from your phone and your partner sees it instantly on theirs.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Receipt images stay on your device — only the extracted text is stored. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

Can I track petrol receipts?

Yes! Supermarkit recognises fuel station receipts and automatically extracts litres, price per litre, and total spend. You'll see a dedicated fuel summary card on each trip, and your fuel spending is tracked over time alongside your grocery data.
